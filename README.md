# 

> 路很长，梦还在。

_ViaLonga, Somniviva._

# 

> 她自无垠雪境而来，
> 
> 将寒霜化作静谧的歌。
> 
> 银发垂至梦的边缘，
> 
> 蓝眸里映着尚未融化的黎明。
> 
> 她行走于风与光的交界，
> 
> 用一片冰晶封存回忆——
> 
> 却仍温柔地，
> 
> 为世界留下了第一场雪。

# 

> She came from the boundless snowfields,
> 
> turning frost into a silent song.
> 
> Silver hair drifts at the edge of dreams,
> 
> blue eyes reflecting an unfrozen dawn.
> 
> She walks between wind and light,
> 
> sealing memories within a crystal of ice—
> 
> yet gently,
> 
> leaves the world its very first snow.
